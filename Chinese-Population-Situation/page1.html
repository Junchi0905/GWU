The Population Situation of China

As we all known, China is the largest country in the world today. Based on United Nations projections China's 2019 population is 1.42 billion.

However, there are some misunderstandings about Chinese population. For example, although China has the largest population, the Chinese government sought to limit population growth by introducing the famous one-child policy, so the growth rate decreased relatively and China has the low birth rate, which combined with its aging population problem.

In this project, we will introduce the basic population situation. It is divided into three parts. First, the population and growth rate will be showed from 1960 to 2015. The situation of each province will also be displayed. Second, the relationship among population, education level, income level and average life span will be introduced by scatter plots. Finally, we will compare the situation from the growth rate and population parts between China and India, the second country in the world.

Our dataset includes the total number of population and growth rate and the population of each province.The second dataset includes mean year for teenagers in school, life expectancy and income level.These three factors will be build a relationship with growth rate to show the correlation between growth rate and life quality. The third dataset of Indian population and growth rate which will be compared with Chinese.

Raw Data can be accessed from the website of World Bank , IMF and Gap Minder.
